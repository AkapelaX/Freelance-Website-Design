Frequently asked questions

Clear answers before we begin.

These are the most common questions businesses ask before starting a website project.

What is included in the $500 website?

A focused custom one-page website for a small business, including responsive design, core business information, service highlights, calls-to-action, social links, maps when needed, one revision round, and launch assistance.

Do I own the finished website?

Yes. Once the project is paid for, you receive the completed website files. Domain, hosting, and third-party service accounts remain under the account holder's control.

Can you redesign an existing website?

Yes. Existing content can often be reorganized and rebuilt into a cleaner, faster, and more modern design.

Are online stores and booking systems included?

Not in the $500 one-page package. Booking tools may be added to larger packages, while e-commerce, user accounts, databases, dashboards, and custom backends require a separate quote based on complexity.

How quickly can the website be completed?

Timing depends on the scope and how quickly content is supplied. A focused static business website can often move from planning to launch quickly once all materials are ready.

Do I have to purchase a monthly maintenance plan?

No. Ongoing support is optional. You may manage the finished website yourself, request individual paid updates, or choose a monthly care plan when regular assistance is more convenient.
